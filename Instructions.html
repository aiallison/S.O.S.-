How To Use S.O.S. :

  1. Create an account
  2. When in need of using S.O.S., click the activate button which has a countdown of 10 minutes
  3. If you need extra time, click the extend time button which will add another 10 minutes to the countdown
  4. If you do NOT click the I'm Safe button and the timer reaches 0:00 it will notify your emergency contact and the police
  5. If S.O.S. is no longer needed, click the I'm Safe button!
